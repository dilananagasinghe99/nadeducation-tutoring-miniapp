Programs by Year Level

Building Strong Foundations

Our primary years program focuses on developing number sense, problem-solving skills, and confidence in mathematics aligned with the Australian Curriculum.

Topics Covered:
  • Number and place value
  • Fractions, decimals and percentages
  • Patterns and algebra introduction
  • Measurement and geometry
  • Statistics and probability basics
  • Problem-solving strategies

Preparing for Senior Mathematics

These critical years build the algebraic thinking and reasoning skills essential for VCE success. We align with Victorian Curriculum standards.

Topics Covered:
  • Algebra and linear equations
  • Pythagoras' theorem and trigonometry
  • Probability and statistics
  • Quadratic relationships
  • Geometric reasoning
  • Index laws and scientific notation
  • Financial mathematics

Year 11 VCE Mathematics

Build a strong foundation for Units 3/4 with our comprehensive Units 1/2 programs.

Mathematical Methods 1/2:
  • Functions and graphs
  • Algebra and logarithms
  • Calculus (differentiation basics)
  • Probability and counting
  • Discrete random variables
General Mathematics 1/2:
  • Computation and practical mathematics
  • Algebra and linear relationships
  • Shape and measurement
  • Graphs and modelling
  • Data analysis and probability
Specialist Mathematics 1/2:
  • Number systems and recursion
  • Algebra and structure
  • Discrete mathematics
  • Geometry and measurement
  • Graphs and networks

Year 12 VCE Mathematics - ATAR Preparation

Intensive preparation for your final VCE exams with comprehensive coverage of all study areas.

General Mathematics 3/4:
  • Data analysis
